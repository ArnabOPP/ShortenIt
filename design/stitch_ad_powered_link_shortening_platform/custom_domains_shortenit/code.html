DNS Configuration Guide

1

Log in to your DNS provider

Access the DNS management console for the domain you want to link (e.g., GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Namecheap).

2

Add the following DNS Records

Choose either an A Record for apex domains or a CNAME for subdomains.

CNAME Record (Subdomains)
Host: go
Value: cname.shortenit.io
A Record (Apex)
Host: @
Value: 76.76.21.21
3

Wait for propagation

DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate globally, though they usually resolve in minutes. Our system will auto-verify once detected.
