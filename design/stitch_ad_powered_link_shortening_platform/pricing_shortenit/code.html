Frequently Asked Questions

How do payouts work for creators? expand_more
Payouts are processed automatically every Friday once your account balance reaches $50. We support PayPal, Stripe, and direct bank transfers in over 40 currencies.
Can I use my own custom domain? expand_more
Yes! Pro plans support up to 3 custom domains, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited domain support. Simply point your DNS records to our servers and start shortening with your brand name.
Is there a limit on API requests? expand_more
Free tier users do not have API access. Pro tier users get 100 requests per second. If you need higher throughput, our Enterprise plan offers customizable rate limits.
What happens if I exceed my monthly link limit? expand_more
On the Free tier, you'll be notified when you hit 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, your existing links will continue to work, but you won't be able to create new ones until the next billing cycle or you upgrade to Pro.
